f_stl2 <- forecast(stl_decomp,h=100)
plot(f_stl2)

* Here, the h is taken to be 100 to understand fucnction better. * This shows us that as time period of forecast increases, the confidence interval also increases. * Which means as the more time we forecast, the more worse our forecast is. Which makes sense.
